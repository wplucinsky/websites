DOUGLAS C. ROGER

Experience

Doug has been a licensed attorney in continuously good standing since October of 1976 in Pennsylvania State and Federal Courts.

In addition to a private law office, Doug is currently the appointed Public Defender for Delaware County, Pennsylvania and heads an office of fifty-nine attorneys and twenty paralegals and support staff.

He was an Assistant County Solicitor for Delaware County, Pennsylvania during the years of 2001-2004. The practice area focused on the representation of dependent children before the State Courts of Pennsylvania.

Doug was the Municipal Solicitor for Nether Providence Township, Delaware County, Pennsylvania a First Class township starting 2001 and ending in 2003. He was the general legal counsel and provided representation to elected commissioners privately and at public sessions.

He was an Assistant District Attorney for Delaware County, Pennsylvania in 1980 ending in 1988. Doug was a trial team leader responsible for criminal trials and preliminary proceedings.
